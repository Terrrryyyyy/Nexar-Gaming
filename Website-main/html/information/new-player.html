New to Server?

Getting you on track to being able to join a unique and entertaining experience, made for YOU.

Step 1: Prepare

Before you join our server, make sure you have done the following...

  • Join our Discord
  • Take a look at our rules

After you have those and have a complete understanding of what we need from you, you are ready to get started.

Step 2: Learn

When you hop into the server, you may be confused as to what you are supposed to do? What are the commands, what are my options?

Civilian Commands

1. /me

Want to pretend to do an action in-game? Want to do something we haven't implemented quite yet?

All you have to do is /me and what you want to act out.

Example: /me Gives TerroristMan43 Glock-19 with minipoints

After taking in all of that information, you are now ready for Step 3.

Step 3: Take Action

You now have what it takes to join the server. If you feel as though you are ready to join a one-and-only experience, meant with YOU in mind, go ahead and join our Discord!
