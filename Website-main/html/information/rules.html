Community Rules

C1. Be respectful to all other players.

C2. Do not force inappropriate RP onto another player. Keep in mind that Aspire Gaming is a twitch streamer friendly community which means you must follow all TOS.

C3. The usage of cheats, exploits and 3rd party applications (including real world trading) to gain an advantage is strictly prohibited.

C4. Derogatory statements are no permitted and will result in a permanent community ban.

C5. Staff decisions are non-negotiable, however, if you believe a decision was made unfairly you can submit a ban appeal on the forums. Lying to staff is not permitted and will result in your ban being extended.

C6. Advertising on any official Aspire platforms is not permitted. If you would like to advertise your stream, talk to a community manager+

gang Rules

G1. Gangs that intend to carry out illegal activities must register via the forums submitting important information such as gang name, and uniforms.

G2. Gangs initially forming will be STRICTLY limited to 8 members, over time as gangs provide good RP they can earn up to a total of 20 members.

G3. Alliances between gangs are permitted however they can only exist to benefit each other in non hostile ways e.g. a trade agreement for households. Gangs can not arrange alliances with other gangs for the purpose of hostile RP

G4. Gangs must be wearing their registered gang attire when interacting with other gangs and when they are in wars.

G5. Gang members can be 'Soft PK'D' when leaving a gang. This essentially means that a gang can force wipe any IC memory's the player had during their time spent in the gang. Gang leaders are not permitted to force a hard PK unless both parties are in agreement.

G6. Gang leaders must settle on the ground rules of a future war before it begins. This includes but is not limited to total members involved, win conditions and the format of the war. Gang staff must be informed of the agreement by both leaders so there are no disagreements in the future.

Role-Playing Rules

F1. Using out of character knowledge in character is strictly prohibited. This includes using information from other characters you may have. (Character slots can be reduce to 1 for people continually violation this rule)

F2. Stream sniping is strictly prohibited.

F3. Players are not to start hostile roleplay for little to no reason. You also must have a valid RP reason to rob someone, an example of this is you CANNOT rob someone for simply talking shit.

F4. Going “OOC” is not permitted. If you are the recipient of poor roleplay or an active rule breach, you must finish the roleplay scenario without breaking character and are required to report the matter after the fact. Threatening staff (or referring to them as government) against a player in roleplay to force them to comply with your wishes is not permitted.

F5. Trolling and griefing is forbidden. This includes injecting yourself into or interrupting other people’s roleplay situations, spamming your microphone/music, baiting police in a manner considered unrealistic and repeatedly killing the same person repeatedly for the same reason does not contribute to roleplay and is considered griefing.

F6. If you have been incapacitated and you respawn at the hospital, your character forgets everything that happened before you were killed, if you have been told police or ems are on their way to your location, you must remain dead and not respawn until they are fishing roleplaying with you.

F7. Logging out during any active roleplay is not allowed (Combat Logging)

F8. Criminals can not rob PD / EMS / Security officers for their WEAPONS, they can however disarm them. Eg /me takes all communication devices and weapons.

F9. Robbery of any organization / jobs stored vehicles, properties, job vehicles, local emergency service vehicles or players’ bank accounts is prohibited. Even WHILE at gunpoint you can not rob these things.

F10. Robberies are not to take place at or near legal job locations or on a person that is using blips such as stores, houses and teleports

F11. Hostages must not be affiliated with the hostage taker

F12. The maximum that can be demand for the group of hostages is 50k

F13. All voice communications must only be done in-game chat, radio or phone. Using discord etc. Is not permitted. In character job meetings might still be held in discord.

F14. Rape, suicide and bomb RP is not permitted.

F15. To some extent, being a corrupt police officer is permitted, but you must have RP justifications for your actions and you CANNOT simply steal weapons from the armoury and sell/distribute them. However, you are NOT allowed to be a corrupt EMS or Security Officer.

F16. Criminal activity is limited to a maximum of 6 players at any given moment.

F17. You may not use video evidence In character unless recorded with a phone or camera. (/e record or /cam). You may not claim that you are wearing a body camera unless you are a police officer and have one attached to your body via your vest.

F18. RDM - Players are not to initiate hostile roleplay for no reason, you must initiate at all times.

F19. Players are not to kidnap Emergency Medical Service Workers at any place or point in time.

F20. Power Gaming - Forcing a desired roleplay outcome/narrative on a situation through methods that cannot be countered towards other players is strictly forbidden.

F21. Bulletproof helmets can only be worn by Police Officers.

F22. VDM - Vehicles are not to be used as weapons unless you are in a active hostile scenario.

F23. Fail Fear - You must value your own life at all times.
